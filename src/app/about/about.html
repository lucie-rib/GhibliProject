About the site

Discover the Magic of Studio Ghibli

Founded in 1985 by visionary directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, Studio Ghibli has captured the hearts of millions worldwide. Explore enchanting worlds, meet unforgettable characters, and dive into timeless stories that celebrate nature, friendship, and imagination.

We invite you to journey through our collection of films, characters, and locations that make Ghibli a true legend in animation.
